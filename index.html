ABOUT US

I travel a lot, especially to China and many European countries. I have tasted a lot international cuisines. Accuse me of being ... too Greek(!), but the taste of pure, virgin olive oil in a freshly cut greek salad, accompanied with olives and hot, fresh bread is something I cannot find anywhere in the world. And it's something I want the world to taste

My country has been mistreated for too long. I want to add a small brick on the wall we ,Greeks,are trying to build, to remind the world that despite all of its problems, Greece was,is and always be a blessed place for anyone who minds his health as the most important virtue in life and wants to be fed with products directly sought from Mother Earth.

Nikos:No intention is more pure, than to share with other people globally the godly gifts of earth.
